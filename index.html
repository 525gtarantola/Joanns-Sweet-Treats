About Us

Nestled in the heart of Old Town, Florida, Joann Demshock-Mango brings sweetness to every corner of her community with her delightful homemade treats. From her cozy home-based business, she whips up delicious confections that are as heartwarming as her love for all things holiday-related! With her husband, Daniel Mango, by her side, Joann's passion for spreading joy through sweets shines every season of the year. Whether it's Christmas, Halloween, or just a Tuesday, there's always a reason to indulge in a little sweetness from Joann’s Sweet Treats!
